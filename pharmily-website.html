How it works

Pharmacy care,
simplified in 3 steps.

No appointments. No waiting rooms. No rushed 2-minute interactions. Just real pharmacist care, whenever you need it.

1
📱

Download & set up

Create your profile, add your medications using the OCR label scanner, and accept HIPAA-compliant consent in under 5 minutes.

2
💊

Track your medications

Pharmily monitors your adherence, surfaces drug interactions, and sends smart reminders — all personalised to your actual prescription list.

3
💬

Talk to your pharmacist

Connect instantly with a licensed PharmD via chat or video — 24/7. Get the counselling you deserve, not a rushed conversation at the pickup counter.

4
🤖

AI MedGuide

Ask anything about your medications at any hour. Our AI gives contextual, personalised guidance — and escalates to a real pharmacist when it matters.

About the founder
NS
Navjot Singh, PharmD
Founder & CEO · Licensed Pharmacist

Built by a pharmacist
who lived the problem.

With over 10 years of retail and clinical pharmacy experience, Navjot Singh saw firsthand how patients were let down — rushed interactions, language barriers, zero after-hours access, and chronic medication non-adherence with no safety net.

Pharmily is his answer. A platform built not by a tech company that spotted a market, but by a licensed pharmacist who understood exactly what was missing — and what it takes to fix it.

"The average retail pharmacy interaction is under two minutes. That's not care — that's a transaction. Patients deserve better."
