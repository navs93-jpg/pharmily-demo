Why Pharmily
Built to close the gap
Retail pharmacy interactions average under 2 minutes. Pharmily gives patients the time and access they deserve.
💬
On-demand consultations
Chat or video with a licensed pharmacist any time — no appointment, no waiting room, no rushed interaction.
🤖
AI MedGuide
Contextual medication guidance powered by AI, personalised to the patient's actual prescription list. Always escalates to a pharmacist when needed.
🌐
Multilingual support
Translated medication instructions in Punjabi, Hindi, Spanish and more — serving communities that are consistently underserved by retail pharmacy.
📋
MTM & billing ready
Structured session notes with CMS-compliant CPT code selection — making MTM reimbursement straightforward for pharmacist partners.
🔒
HIPAA compliant
Encrypted data at rest and in transit, role-based access controls, and comprehensive audit logging from day one.
📈
Adherence tracking
Patients log doses, view trends, and share reports with their pharmacist — closing the loop between counselling and real-world behaviour.
